Day:-1 : Arrival At Jaipur


Pickup from Jaipur by placard carrying driver and drive straight to Jaipur transfer to Hotel Later visit to the fabled Pink City, the Jaipur of old. Drive through the Johari Bazaar to the Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds). Visit the various museums inside the City Palace and the ancient observatory, Jantar Mantar. Pay a visit to the Govind Deoji Temple, housing the patron deity of Jaipur. Evening free for your own activities like shopping etc.


Day:-2 : Jaipur Sightseeing


On the second day of the trip, visit the magnificent Amber Palace. See the Largest Cannon on Wheels in the World, and old cannon foundry at Jaigarh. Visit to the Kanak Vrindavan Temple in the valley of flowers. Evening visit Chokhi Dhani for Rajasthani traditional Dinner with dance and Music. Overnight stay at Hotel.


Day:-3 : Jaipur-Fatehpur Sikri-Agra


After breakfast drive to Agra on the way Visit Abhaneri Step Wells and Fetehpur Sikri. Check-in at hotel. Overnight stay in Agra.


Tourist Places:


Fetehpur Sikri – Buland Darwaza, Tomb of Salim Chisti, Diwani–i-Am, Diwan–i-Khas, Jodha Bai’s House.


Day:- 4 : Agra


After breakfast at hotel full day sightseeing of Agra, visit famous Taj Mahal and Red Fort. Overnight stay at Hotel in Agra


Tourist Places:


Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Jama Masjid, Dayal Bagh, Akbar Mausoleum, Octagonal Tower


Day:- 5 : Agra-Sikandra-New Delhi


Breakfast at Hotel, later drive to New Delhi on the way stop a Sikandra. Overnight stay at Hotel in New Delhi.


Tourist Places:


Sikandra Fort


Day:- 6 : Departure


After Breakfast proceeds for Full day sightseeing of New Delhi. In evening dropping off at airport for your home destination. (tour ends).
